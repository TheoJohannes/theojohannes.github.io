Devil's Advocate

“Sometimes you need a new perspective on life. With Devil’s Advocate you can solve your problems easily. Just look through the window and watch life change before your eyes.”

The idea for Devil’s Advocate came from the concept that there is more than one side to every argument. The package is meant to intrigue the consumer and encourage critical thinking by drawing them in with imagery and colours associated with typically rejected or feared perspectives. The idiom “playing Devil’s advocate” tends to be seen as a negative point of view, but this packaging is playing Devil’s Advocate on that idea. I purposely chose a children’s toy package to emulate a tongue-in-cheek, satyrical and even playful twist on the expected.
